Parallel Mobile App

Introducing "Parallel" - the revolutionary mobile app that simplifies the way drivers find and pay for available pay parking spots. Parallel is designed to make parking effortless and stress-free, ensuring that drivers can easily locate and pay for parking spaces with just a few taps on their phone.

With Parallel, drivers can instantly search for available pay parking spots nearby using their phone's GPS. The app provides real-time updates on parking availability, including the number of available spots, rates, and time restrictions. Users can filter and sort parking options based on their preferences, such as location, price, and duration
